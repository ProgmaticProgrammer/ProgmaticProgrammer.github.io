CHEAT SHEET

  1. Reduce interaction cost of your interface.
  2. Lower cognitive load to encourage use.
  3. Increase processing fluency of your content.
  4. Interrupt hedonic adaptation with novelty.
  5. Deliver valuable unpredictable rewards.
